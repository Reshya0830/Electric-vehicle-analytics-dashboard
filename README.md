⚡ Electric Vehicle Analytics Dashboard

The Electric Vehicle Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project that visualizes EV market trends and sustainability insights. It provides a data-driven view of key metrics such as average battery capacity, CO₂ savings, resale value, and maintenance cost across various regions and vehicle types.

Key Features:

Interactive dashboard analyzing EV performance and market dynamics.

Visual insights into CO₂ reduction, resale trends, and battery health.

Regional and vehicle-type comparisons for deeper sustainability insights.

Data-driven decision support for understanding EV efficiency and adoption patterns.

Tools & Technologies:

Power BI for visualization and analytics

Excel for data cleaning and preparation

Data Modeling & DAX for calculated measures and KPIs

Outcome:
Delivers a comprehensive understanding of electric vehicle market trends, highlighting cost efficiency, environmental impact, and performance analytics for strategic decision-making.
